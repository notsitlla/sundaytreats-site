Common questions

FAQ

How do I place an order?

Simply send us an email at sundaytreatllc@gmail.com with your desired items, quantity, and date needed. We'll get back to you quickly to confirm and finalize details.

How much notice do you need?

We ask for at least 48–72 hours notice for standard orders. Custom or large event orders may require more lead time — reach out early and we'll do our best!

What areas do you serve?

We serve Littleton, Highlands Ranch, and the greater Denver metro area. Contact us to confirm delivery or pickup options for your location.

Do you do recurring office orders?

Yes! We love working with local businesses on regular treat schedules. Ask us about setting up a weekly or monthly standing order for your team.

What's included in a cookie platter?

Platters include an assortment of our freshly baked cookies — flavors vary by season. Let us know if you have any preferences, dietary needs, or favorites.

Can I customize my order?

Absolutely. From flavors to packaging and presentation, we're happy to tailor your order for your event or recipient. Just tell us what you have in mind.
